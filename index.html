About Me

I have always been deeply interested in computer science and mathematics, and am continually looking to learn new technologies and skills. I started teaching myself to code in 2015, after feeling unchallenged by high school's computing class, and have continued to improve my skills as a developer considerably through a variety of hobby projects. These projects range from games, to apps, to utility programs, through which I like to challenge myself to not only improve in my general abilities, but also to learn new technologies and concepts.

I graduated Magna cum Laude with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Minor in Mathematics from Weber State University in 2023.
